# Privacy Policy for Squiggle

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
Squiggle is designed to operate entirely locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or process any of your personal data or user-generated content on our servers.

### 1.1. Local Storage
All data created or modified within the application (such as plays, formations, and drafts) is stored locally on your device using your browser's Local Storage or a local database running on your machine. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it manually.

### 1.2. Analytics and Tracking
Squiggle does not use any third-party analytics, tracking scripts, or telemetry services. We do not track your usage of the application.

## 2. Permissions
The Squiggle browser extension requires the following permissions to function:

*   **Storage**: Used to save your preferences and application state locally.

## 3. Data Sharing
Since all data is stored locally, we do not have access to your data and therefore cannot share it with third parties.
